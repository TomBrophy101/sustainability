Game of sea life knowledge


The purpose of this website is to highlight the sustainability of ocean life. This will touch on the many different kinds of beautiful sea animals that are part of the ocean. It also highlights the issues that plague these creatures. It also includes a sign in sector if you want to sign up and help the cause. Finally, it includes a game that will test your knowledge about how much you know about sea life and ocean animals in general. We also have two separate pages that will show you two pages about the game.
